Title: Lacking light
Genre: Roguelike
Variant: Top Down
Main mechanic: MC uses a lamp, as time passes lightning decreases, gotta keep getting oil to recharge it.
Lose condition: If lamp turns off you lose, also enemies follow you, it they touch you you lose light.
Win condition: Torch the main bonfire to light up the entire level (and go to the next).

There will be a finite number of tile layouts for the level, they'll be put at random to generate a different level every time.
You'll have keys to open the doors and gain access.

Mandatory:
Title screen
Start game
Credits screen
Print controls

Optional: 
Volume control
Tutorial

Aesthetic: Pixelart 8x8
Portview size: 240x135
Color palette: Purplemorning-8
Music: Ambient dark
